Efficacy of Paper Use

2018

18 x 24 in poster.

An information graphic analyzing the efficacy of paper use in making modular paper structures. These twenty-five paper modules were constructed from 6 x 6 in squares of paper and then evaluated based on complexity, efficacy of paper use, and whether or not it contained any curves.

Modules were plotted onto a plane according to their assigned values in each category and then photographed, showing the relationship of folds, paper, and structural integrity.
