Breaking and Building the System

2017

36 x 48 in poster, mobile app prototype,
4.5 x 9 in booklet.

A three-part project to promote a mock lecture series featuring talks from six contemporary designers. The isometric typographic structure that binds the three different forms is inspired by the structure of a construction platform on top of a highrise building. Each of the talks confronts the problems that exist in our cultural systems and inspire and promote an attitude to resolve these issues: they break down then build up the system.

In the mobile prototype of the lecture series’ app, users are able to navigate through the app by tapping and swiping through the isometric grid to read more about the speakers and find the ticket and location information.

The third component of the project is an accordion fold booklet to promote one of the speakers of the series, Keller Easterling. It includes one of her essays, “No You’re Not”, arranged into an angled and mountainous twelve-page fold out.
