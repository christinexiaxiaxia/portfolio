/RANDOM_INDEX: The Works of Sulki and Min

2018

8 x 10 in brochures.

This 12-page brochure is an accompanying piece for a mock-exhibition "/RANDOM_INDEX" for the designers Sulki and Min. The booklet features some of their works alongside interview questions and answers. Based off of their website that features randomly generated galleries of their works and even their names in the top left corner switching between English and Korean when refreshed, the placement, size, leading, and font of text was determined by sets of rules based off of number of words, beginning letters, and other values in each paragraph.

Sulki and Min's design often revolves around interchangeable elements and adaptability into different formats and aspect ratios, so the colour scheme and printing was also kept in mind while arranging the images and text.

The booklets can be printed in different variations of colours and paper while still being legibile, allowing the content to be printed for colour or black and white, and also on varying qualities of paper.
