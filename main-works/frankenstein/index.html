Frankenstein

2017

6 x 9 in perfect bound book, subi and durotone pages, cardstock cover.

A hand-bound copy of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley’s Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus with an introduction of Donna Haraway’s A Cyborg Manifesto. This book design translates Shelley’s literary classic into contemporary times; Haraway’s essay establishes a more modern context for the novel, as does the fragmented and transitioning type as it shifts from serif, to sans-serif, to monospaced. The book’s introduction is printed on a cream duotone with the rest of the novel printed on a toothier subi paper.
