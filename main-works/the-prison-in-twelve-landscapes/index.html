The Prison in Twelve Landscapes

2018

11 x 17 in risograph posters.

Posters designed for a documentary screening and Q&A hosted at OCAD University on March 27th for Brett Story's "The Prison in Twelve Landscapes". These were made with a larger encompassing series of lectures in mind, related by the idea of assembling people, stories, and structures.

The isometric and zigzagging letterforms are derived from the sprawling urban skyline and the intersecting shapes of the reflections of skyscrapers in the windows of other skyscrapers. The relationship of these forms and structures work in a similar way to the relationships of the people and environments that Story includes in her documentary; all from diverse backgrounds and serving different purposes, but still all part of the same system.
